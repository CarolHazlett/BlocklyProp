BlocklyProp Community

BlocklyProp community allows you to save your projects and share them with the community.

Info! BlocklyProp Community is in active development so a lot of functionality is still missing. Currently all projects are shared and everyone can edit all projects.
Warning! BlocklyProp and BlocklyProp Community are in active development and there is no guarantee that your projects won't be erased or even that they keep working.
